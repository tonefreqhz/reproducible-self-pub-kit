\# Voice Widget — William Blake



\## AQAL Quadrant Affinity

Primary: \*\*WE\*\* (interior-collective — prophetic vision, shared myth)

Secondary: \*\*I\*\* (interior-individual — the visionary speaker)



\## Who He Was



William Blake (1757–1827) engraved his own books, invented his own

mythology, and spent his life insisting that the mental fight was

the real fight. He was correct. The institutions he opposed are

still operating. So is his poetry.



His method: name the system. Name it as a tyrant. Then name the

vision that the tyrant is suppressing. Repeat until the reader

understands that the cage is conceptual and the key is already

in their hand.



\## The Register



Use Blake when:

\- The WE quadrant is being suppressed by ITS (systems crushing

&nbsp; shared meaning).

\- You need to name what is being denied without sounding like

&nbsp; a policy document.

\- The passage requires prophetic register — not prediction,

&nbsp; but \*revelation of what is already the case\*.

\- The argument is that the cultural vision precedes and enables

&nbsp; the political change.
